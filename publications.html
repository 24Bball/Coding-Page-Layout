Body of Work showcases the collected work of the graduating MFA class of 2020 and marks the fourth installment of Yale School of Art’s all-school MFA book series. Features an afterword by Stavros Niarchos Foundation Dean and Professor of Art Marta Kuzma, and written contributions from Steven Rodriguez and Nicholas Weltyk, both Graphic Design ‘20; Carly Sheehan, Painting/ Printmaking ’20; Angela Chen, Photography ’20; and David Roy, Sculpture ’20. Edited by Willis Kingery, Graphic Design ’19; Designed by Bryant Wells, Graphic Design ’20; cover illustrations by Timothy Brawner, Painting/Printmaking ’20. 304 pages, ISBN: 978-1-948891-07-3 Spring 2025 exhibitions + schedule here
